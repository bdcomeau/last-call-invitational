1 · How Parimutuel Betting Works

Unlike fixed-odds betting, parimutuel betting pools all wagers together. The house takes a percentage (if set), and the remainder is split among winning bettors proportional to their stake. Odds are determined by where everyone bets — not set in advance.

The Three Pools

PoolYou win when your team…
WINFinishes 1st
PLACEFinishes 1st or 2nd
SHOWFinishes 1st, 2nd, or 3rd

WIN, PLACE, and SHOW are separate pools — money wagered on WIN stays in the WIN pool and is only paid out to WIN bettors, and so on.

Payout Formula

Your payout = (your stake ÷ total winning stakes) × net pool. Payouts are floored to whole dollars — fractions of a dollar go to the house.

💡 Example: WIN pool nets $500. You bet $10 on Alpha to WIN. Total WIN stakes on Alpha = $40. Your payout = (10 ÷ 40) × $500 = $125.

2 · Installing on Your iPhone

The app is a web app that installs to your Home Screen and works offline once loaded.

⚠️ Install to your Home Screen before using in a live event. If you just open it in a browser tab, your data can be wiped by iOS at any time. Always launch from the Home Screen icon.

How to install:

  • Open the app link in Safari on your iPhone (must be Safari — not Chrome).
  • Tap the Share button (square with arrow) in the Safari toolbar.
  • Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  • Rename if you like, then tap Add in the top-right corner.
  • Launch from the new Home Screen icon — it opens full-screen.
Once installed and opened at least once, the app works without a signal. All data is stored on your device — nothing is sent to any server.

Keeping the App Current

The app checks for updates automatically — 3 seconds after loading, then every 5 minutes. When an update is available a gold banner appears at the top of the screen.

  • Update Now — clears the service worker cache and reloads the latest version.
  • Later — dismisses the banner for this session. Tap it between events, not during a live one.

3 · Quick Start — First Event

  1. Create an event — tap EVENTS → enter a name → "+ Create Event".
  2. Add teams — the Event Setup panel appears below. Type each team name → "+ Add Team". Or import from CSV.
  3. Add bettors — tap BETTORS → enter name, email, phone → "+ Add Bettor". Or import from CSV.
  4. Accept bets — tap PLACE BETS → select a bettor → fill in WIN / PLACE / SHOW amounts → "✓ Submit Bets".
  5. Collect cash — confirm the total in the review modal → tap "💵 Cash Received — Confirm".
  6. Record ticket — write the generated ticket number (LCI-2026-XXXX) on the bettor's physical sheet.
  7. Enter results — tap RESULTS → select 1st, 2nd, 3rd place teams → payouts calculate instantly.
  8. Pay out winners — use "Open & Print Winner Summaries" to generate payout slips.

6 · Place Bets Tab

Entering a New Bet

  1. Select a bettor from the dropdown. Their previously submitted tickets appear below.
  2. Fill in the Enter New Bet grid — all teams are shown with WIN / PLACE / SHOW dropdowns. Live odds appear below each team name. Leave a dropdown at $0 to skip that pool.
  3. Check the totals row at the bottom — WIN / PLACE / SHOW subtotals and Grand Total update as you go.
  4. Tap "✓ Submit Bets" — a review modal appears showing every bet and the total to collect.
  5. Collect cash from the bettor, then tap "💵 Cash Received — Confirm".
  6. A ticket number (e.g. LCI-2026-0042) is generated. Write it on the bettor's physical paper sheet.
  7. Optionally tap "📤 Share / Print Receipt" to send the bettor a digital copy.

Betting Sheets

Print blank paper sheets for bettors to fill in and hand back with cash. Select the number of copies → "🖨 Prepare Sheets" → open in Safari → Print or AirDrop.

Importing Bets

Bottom of the Place Bets screen → Choose File. See Section 11 for CSV format.

⚠️ Once all three placements are entered on the Results tab, betting is locked. No new bets can be entered or imported.

7 · View Wagers Tab (Wager Management)

Bettor Ticket / Receipt

Select a bettor → tap "🖨 Print Ticket" to view their receipt — all bets grouped by ticket number with subtotals and grand total. Tap "📤 Share / Print" to open the iOS share sheet: choose iMessage, AirDrop, Mail, Print, or Save to Files.

Tote Board

Live pool totals: Total collected, WIN pool, PLACE pool, SHOW pool, and the net payout line after handle.

Live Odds (collapsible)

Tap the "📊 LIVE ODDS" header to expand or collapse. Shows estimated return per $1 bet for each team across WIN / PLACE / SHOW. Updates in real time as bets are placed.

💡 How to read odds: 4.2× means a $10 bet returns approximately $42. Lower odds = more popular team = more competition for the pool.

Wager List

All bets sorted A–Z by bettor name. Use the filter bar at the top to search by:

  • Bettor name (e.g. "Bruce")
  • Team name (e.g. "Alpha")
  • Bet type (e.g. "win")
  • Ticket number (e.g. "0042" finds LCI-2026-0042)

🖨 Report

Generates a full Wagers Report — pool summary, wagers by team, and all bets by bettor grouped by ticket. Available any time during betting. Opens in Safari for printing or sharing.

Voiding a Bet

Tap ✕ on any bet row. Locked ticket bets (those with a LCI-XXXX number) require triple confirmation, since voiding any bet on a ticket voids the entire ticket.

9 · Field Bets

A Field bet covers every team simultaneously — if ANY team finishes in the position you bet on, you win. Select 🌐 Field from the team list in the bet entry grid.

What You Pay

The amount you enter is per team. A $5 Field WIN bet with 30 teams = $150 collected from the bettor ($5 × 30). The amount hint on the bet entry screen shows this clearly: "Collect $150 from bettor ($5 × 30 teams)"

Field Bet Odds

Field bets have lower odds than most individual teams — sometimes lower than even the favourite. Here's why: the full $150 enters the WIN pool as the field bettor's stake. The payout formula is your stake ÷ total winning stakes × net pool, so the large stake denominator results in a proportionally modest payout per dollar risked.

💡 Think of it like betting the field at a horse race — you're nearly guaranteed to win, but you collect much less per dollar because you're competing against yourself across all teams.

13 · Payout Models

Set in Event Setup (Events tab) before betting begins. The model affects how PLACE and SHOW pools are split among winners.

ModelPLACE PoolSHOW Pool
SimplifiedSplit 50/50 — half to 1st-place bettors, half to 2nd-place bettorsSplit into thirds — one third each to 1st, 2nd, 3rd place bettors
True RacetrackEntire pool split among ALL bettors who picked 1st OR 2nd finisherEntire pool split among ALL bettors who picked 1st, 2nd, OR 3rd finisher
💡 Simplified is generally fairer for small tournaments where only a few bettors picked the winner. True Racetrack more closely mimics professional horse racing pools.

All individual payouts are floored to whole dollars — any remaining cents go to the house automatically.

16 · FAQ & Troubleshooting

Can I add bets after the event has started?

Yes — bets can be added any time until the final placements (1st / 2nd / 3rd) are entered on the Results tab. Once all three are set, betting is locked and import is blocked.

Can I fix a wrong bet amount?

Void the bet on the View Wagers tab (tap ✕), then re-enter it correctly. Locked ticket bets require triple confirmation before voiding — it voids the entire ticket.

Can I run multiple events?

Yes — each event is completely independent with its own teams, bettors, and bets. Switch between events on the Events tab. Only one event can be active at a time.

What if I accidentally delete data?

Data stored in your device's localStorage cannot be recovered once deleted. Export a backup of important events by duplicating them (Events → ⧉ Duplicate) so you always have a copy.

The app shows "Update available" during the event

Tap Later to dismiss the banner. Apply updates between events to avoid any interruption to live betting.

The placement dropdowns don't show any teams

Teams must be added first via Event Setup (Events tab → Event Setup → Add Teams). If no teams exist, the dropdowns will be empty.

A bettor's name shows "?" in results

This happens when a bet references a bettor ID that no longer exists (e.g. the bettor was deleted). Restore the bettor on the Bettors tab with the same name, or note it in your manual records.

The Field bet payout seems too low

That's correct — see Section 9. A field bet backs all 30 teams, so a $5 bet contributes $150 to the pool. The large stake in the denominator means a lower return per dollar, similar to betting a heavy favourite.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage under the key lci-v4. It never leaves your device and is never sent to any server. Do not clear browser/website data while running a live event.

The app is frozen / buttons don't respond

Force-close the app (swipe up in the iOS app switcher) and reopen from the Home Screen icon. If it persists, check the version number (shown at the top-right beside the ? button) and use the Update banner if an update is available.
