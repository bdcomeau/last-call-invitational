Parimutuel Betting System

Last Call Invitational

A complete parimutuel betting management system for golf tournaments and scrambles. Manage bettors, accept wagers, calculate live odds, and pay out winners — all from your phone.

Overview

The Last Call Invitational app is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that runs entirely in your browser. No internet connection required after installation. All data is stored locally on your device.

How Parimutuel Betting Works

Unlike fixed-odds betting, parimutuel betting pools all wagers together. The house takes a percentage, and the remainder is split among winning bettors proportional to their stake. This means odds are determined by where everyone bets — not set in advance.

PoolWho Wins
WINBettors who picked the 1st place team
PLACEBettors who picked 1st or 2nd place team
SHOWBettors who picked 1st, 2nd, or 3rd place team

Events Tab

Creating an Event

Enter an event name and tap "+ Create Event". The event becomes active immediately and the Event Setup panel appears below the events list.

Event Setup (inline)

When an active, non-archived event is selected, the setup panel shows:

  • Event Name — edit the display name at any time
  • Payout Model — Simplified or True Racetrack (see Payout Models)
  • Add Teams — add individually or import CSV (one name per row)
  • Reset Event Data — slide the red button to wipe all teams, bets and bettors (cannot be undone)

Events List

Tap the count/▲ to collapse the events list and make more room. Each event shows:

  • Open — make an inactive event active
  • Archive — closes betting on an event (results still viewable)
  • Results — view results for an archived event
  • ⧉ Duplicate — create a full copy with a new name (all teams, bettors, bets, placements)
  • 🗑 Delete — permanently remove an event
💡 Tip: Use Duplicate to create test events without starting from scratch.

View Wagers Tab

This is Wager Management — your live view of all betting activity.

Bettor Ticket / Receipt

Select a bettor → tap "🖨 Print Ticket" to view their receipt showing all bets grouped by ticket number. Tap "📤 Share / Print" to open the iOS share sheet (iMessage, AirDrop, Mail, Print, Save to Files).

Tote Board

Shows live pool totals: Total, WIN pool, PLACE pool, SHOW pool, and the handle/net payout line.

Live Odds

Tap the "📊 LIVE ODDS" header to expand/collapse. Shows estimated return per $1 bet for each team across WIN / PLACE / SHOW. Updates in real time as bets are entered.

💡 How to read odds: 4.2× means a $10 bet would return approximately $42. Lower odds = more popular team. Field bet odds reflect the full pooled stake of all field bettors across all teams.

Wager List

All bets sorted A-Z by bettor name. Use the filter bar to search by bettor name, team name, bet type, or ticket number (e.g. type "0042" to find ticket LCI-2026-0042).

🖨 Report

Generates a full Wagers Report — pool summary, wagers by team, and all bets by bettor grouped by ticket. Opens in Safari tab for printing or sharing. Available any time during betting.

Results Tab

Final Placements

Select the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place finishing teams from the dropdowns. Once all three are set, the full results section appears automatically below.

House Take

Set before viewing payouts. Two options:

  • % of Pool — slide 0–10% to retain a percentage of the total pool
  • Flat $ — enter a fixed dollar amount to retain

House take resets to 0% each time you open the Results tab. Adjust it before printing reports.

Pool Summary

Shows Total Collected, House Retained, Win/Place/Show net pools, and Total Paid Out.

Bettor Reconciliation

Each bettor's bets, win/loss status, and net gain or loss. Tap "🖨 Full Report" to generate the complete reconciliation report including final odds, pool summary, winners sorted by payout, and all bets by bettor A-Z.

Winner Payout Summary

Shows exactly what each winning bettor receives. Tap "Open & Print Winner Summaries" to generate printable payout sheets for handing out winnings.

Field Bets

A Field bet covers every team — if ANY team wins, the field bettor wins. Select "🌐 Field" from the team list when entering bets.

⚠️ Field bets have lower odds because the bettor's stake is multiplied across all teams in the pool. A $5 Field WIN bet with 30 teams contributes $150 to the WIN pool. This makes field a low-odds, "safe" bet — similar to betting a heavy favourite.

The amount hint shows exactly what to collect: "Collect $150 from bettor ($5 × 30 teams)"

Ticket Numbers

Every confirmed bet sheet gets a unique ticket number in the format LCI-2026-XXXX where:

  • LCI — Last Call Invitational prefix
  • 2026 — year the event was created
  • XXXX — 4-digit sequential number (0001, 0002, etc.)

Ticket numbers are guaranteed unique — the app checks all existing tickets before issuing a new one.

Recording Ticket Numbers

After cash is confirmed, write the ticket number on the bettor's physical paper betting sheet. The app displays a reminder: "Record this ticket number on the physical betting sheet and retain for the house's records."

Payout Models

ModelPLACE PoolSHOW PoolBest For
SimplifiedSplit 50/50 between 1st-place bettors and 2nd-place bettorsSplit equally into thirds (1st, 2nd, 3rd place bettors)Small tournaments, casual events
True RacetrackEntire pool split among ALL bettors who picked 1st OR 2nd finisherEntire pool split among ALL bettors who picked 1st, 2nd, OR 3rd finisherLarger events, traditional parimutuel

Set the payout model in Event Setup (Events tab) before betting begins. All payouts are rounded down to whole dollars — any remainder goes to the house.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add bets after the event starts?

Yes — bets can be added at any time until the final placements (1st/2nd/3rd) are entered on the Results tab. Once all three are set, betting is locked.

What happens if I delete a bettor?

You cannot delete a bettor who has active bets. Remove their bets on the View Wagers tab first, then delete the bettor.

Can I run multiple events?

Yes — each event is completely independent with its own teams, bettors, and bets. Switch between events on the Events tab. Only one event can be active at a time.

How do I fix a wrong bet?

Go to View Wagers → find the bet → tap ✕ to void it. Locked ticket bets (those with ticket numbers) require triple confirmation before voiding, as it voids the entire ticket.

What if I lose data?

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Clearing browser data or using private/incognito mode will erase it. Do not clear site data while running a live event.

The app says "Update available" — should I update during an event?

Tap "Later" to dismiss during an active event. Apply updates between events to avoid any interruption.

What is the Field bet?

The Field bet backs every team simultaneously. If any team finishes in the position you bet on (WIN/PLACE/SHOW), you win. However, field bets typically return lower odds because all field bettors compete against each other across every team in the pool.
