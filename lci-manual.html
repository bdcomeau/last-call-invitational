1 · How Parimutuel Betting Works

Unlike fixed-odds betting, parimutuel betting pools all wagers together. The house takes a percentage (if set), and the remainder is split among winning bettors proportional to their stake. Odds are determined by where everyone bets — not set in advance.

The Three Pools

PoolYou win when your team…
WINFinishes 1st
PLACEFinishes 1st or 2nd
SHOWFinishes 1st, 2nd, or 3rd

Payout Formula

Your payout = (your stake ÷ total winning stakes) × net pool. Payouts are floored to whole dollars.

💡 Example: WIN pool nets $500. You bet $10 on Alpha to WIN. Total WIN stakes on Alpha = $40. Your payout = (10 ÷ 40) × $500 = $125.

A Note on Odds

It is mathematically possible for a team's SHOW odds to be higher than their WIN odds. This happens when a team is heavily backed to WIN but few people bet them to SHOW — less competition in the SHOW pool means a higher return per dollar. This information is valuable — keep the Live Odds screen passcode-protected.

2 · Installing on Your iPhone

⚠️ Install to your Home Screen before using at a live event. Opening in a browser tab risks data loss. Always launch from the Home Screen icon.
  1. Open the app URL in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome).
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow) in the Safari toolbar.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Rename if you like, then tap Add.
  5. Launch from the Home Screen icon — it opens full-screen.

Auto Updates

The app checks for updates automatically — 3 seconds after loading, then every 5 minutes. A gold banner appears when an update is ready.

  • Update Now — clears cache and reloads the latest version.
  • Later — dismisses for this session. Update between events, not during.
The version number is shown in gold at the top-right of every screen beside the ? button.

3 · Quick Start — First Event

  1. Create an event — tap EVENTS → enter a name → "+ Create Event".
  2. Add teams — the Event Setup panel appears below. Type each team name → "+ Add Team". Or import from CSV.
  3. Add bettors — tap BETTORS → enter name, email, phone → "+ Add Bettor". Or import from CSV.
  4. Accept bets — tap PLACE BETS → select a bettor → fill in WIN / PLACE / SHOW amounts → "✓ Submit Bets".
  5. Collect cash — confirm the total in the review modal → tap "💵 Cash Received — Confirm".
  6. Record ticket — write the generated ticket number (LCI-2026-XXXX) on the bettor's physical sheet.
  7. Enter results — tap RESULTS → select 1st, 2nd, 3rd place teams → payouts calculate instantly.
  8. Pay out winners — use "Open & Print Winner Summaries" to generate payout slips.

6 · Place Bets Tab

Entering a New Bet

  1. Select a bettor from the dropdown. Their submitted tickets appear below.
  2. Fill in the Enter New Bet grid — all teams listed with WIN / PLACE / SHOW dropdowns. Live odds appear below each team name. Leave at $0 to skip.
  3. Tap "✓ Submit Bets" — a review modal shows every bet and the total to collect.
  4. Collect cash, then tap "💵 Cash Received — Confirm".
  5. A ticket number (e.g. LCI-2026-0042) is generated. Write it on the bettor's paper sheet.
  6. Optionally tap "📤 Share / Print Receipt" to send the bettor a digital copy.
⚠️ Once 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place are entered on the Results tab, betting is locked. No new bets can be entered or imported.

Live Odds (passcode protected)

The Live Odds section is collapsed by default and requires a passcode to open. It re-locks automatically every time you leave the Wagers tab.

⚠️ Live Odds reveal valuable strategic information — who to bet on, which pools offer value, and when SHOW odds exceed WIN odds on the same team. Keep the passcode confidential and do not share the screen with bettors.

Tap the "📊 LIVE ODDS" header → enter the passcode → odds expand. Shows estimated return per $1 for each team across WIN / PLACE / SHOW, updated in real time.

💡 How to read odds: 4.2× means a $10 bet returns approximately $42. Lower odds = more popular team = more competition for that pool slice.

8 · Results Tab

Final Placements

Select 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place teams from the dropdowns at the top. Once all three are set, the full results section appears automatically.

House Take

Set before reviewing payouts. The house take is deducted proportionally from each pool — WIN, PLACE, and SHOW each contribute their share based on their size relative to the total. This is fairer than taking it all from one pool.

ModeHow it works
% of PoolRetain that percentage of the total pool, split proportionally across WIN/PLACE/SHOW
Flat $Retain a fixed dollar amount, split proportionally across WIN/PLACE/SHOW
💡 House take is saved once you tap "Prepare Winner Summaries". If you return to this event later, your house take setting is restored automatically.

Pool Summary

Total Collected, House Retained, Win / Place / Show net pools, and Total Paid Out — all rounded to whole dollars.

Bettor Reconciliation + Full Report

Every bettor's bets, win/loss, and net gain/loss. Tap "🖨 Full Report" for the complete printable report.

Winner Payout Summary

Exactly what each winner is owed. Tap "🏆 Prepare Winner Summaries" then "Open & Print" for printable payout slips.

9 · Field Bets

A Field bet covers every team simultaneously — if ANY team finishes in the position you bet on, you win. Select 🌐 Field from the team list.

What You Pay

The amount you enter is per team. A $5 Field WIN bet with 30 teams = $150 collected ($5 × 30). The amount hint shows this clearly.

Field Bet Odds

Field bets have lower odds than most individual teams. The full amount ($150) enters the pool as the field bettor's stake. The payout formula stake ÷ total winning stakes × net pool means the large denominator produces a modest return per dollar risked.

💡 Think of it like betting the field at a horse race — you're nearly guaranteed to win, but you collect much less per dollar because you're competing against yourself across all teams.

Field Odds Display

The Live Odds table shows field odds as the weighted average across all teams — reflecting the typical return a field bettor would receive if a representative team wins.

13 · Payout Models

ModelPLACE PoolSHOW Pool
SimplifiedSplit 50/50 — half to 1st-place bettors, half to 2nd-place bettorsSplit into thirds — one third each to 1st, 2nd, 3rd place bettors
True RacetrackEntire pool split among ALL bettors who picked 1st OR 2nd finisherEntire pool split among ALL bettors who picked 1st, 2nd, OR 3rd finisher
💡 Simplified is generally fairer for small tournaments. True Racetrack mimics professional horse racing pools.

All payouts floored to whole dollars. Fractions go to the house. House take is deducted proportionally from each pool before payouts are calculated.

16 · FAQ & Troubleshooting

Can I add bets after the event starts?

Yes — until all three placements are entered on the Results tab. Once set, betting is locked.

Can I rename a team after bets are placed?

Yes — tap ✏️ Rename on any team in Event Setup. The new name appears everywhere immediately because all bets reference the team by ID, not name.

Why can't I delete a team?

Teams with active bets cannot be deleted. Remove those bets on View Wagers first.

Can I fix a wrong bet amount?

Void the bet on View Wagers (tap ✕), then re-enter it. Locked ticket bets require triple confirmation — voiding any bet on a ticket voids the entire ticket.

The house take reset when I switched events

House take is saved to the event once you tap "Prepare Winner Summaries". Before that it resets to 0% each visit so you always set it deliberately. Tap Prepare to lock it in.

Why does SHOW pay more than WIN on the same team?

The WIN and SHOW pools are separate. If a team is heavily backed to WIN but few people bet them to SHOW, the SHOW stake is small and the return per dollar is higher. This is mathematically correct and is intentional parimutuel behaviour.

What does the field odds number represent?

The weighted average return across all teams — approximately what you'd collect per dollar if a typical team wins. Field bets generally have lower odds than individual teams because the large pooled stake reduces the return.

The app is frozen / buttons don't respond

Force-close the app (swipe up in the iOS app switcher) and reopen from the Home Screen icon. Check the version number (gold, top-right) and update if a banner appeared.

Where is my data stored?

All data is in your browser's localStorage under key lci-v4. It never leaves your device. Do not clear browser data during a live event.
